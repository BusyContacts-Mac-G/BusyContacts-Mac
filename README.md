## Overview

BusyContacts Professional Suite is a complete contact relationship management platform that serves professionals with the organization depth, integration breadth, and interaction history that serious professional contact management requires.

BusyCal integration surfaces calendar events involving any contact directly within the contact record, connecting the scheduling and contact management sides of professional relationship management in a single unified context.

Custom fields and contact templates allow your contact database to capture the specific information relevant to your professional context beyond the standard fields, making BusyContacts Professional Suite adaptable to any industry or workflow.

Duplicate detection and merge tools maintain contact database hygiene as it grows, identifying and combining duplicate records before they create confusion and inconsistency across connected calendar and communication applications.

BusyContacts Professional Suite is the contact management platform for professionals who recognize that their contact database is a professional asset and want a tool that manages it with the organization, integration, and relationship context that its value warrants.

## Performance & Integration

BusyContacts is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while BusyContacts processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
